About Pastry Square

Welcome to Pastry Square, a sweet, sour, and delectable pastry company that focuses on the satisfaction of our customers. We are a self made business that has been making pastries, breads, and other treats for years trying to gain popularity around the city. Our creation and idea came when many of our owners took culinary school and fell in love with the art of food.

We want to make sure that all of our customers are satisfied with what we are selling. We are the perfect company for any situation, whether it is for your wedding, a birthday party, or simply just a dessert, we are here to meet your cravings. We always believe in efficiency, so that is why we are trying to branch out our name throughout the city so we can proivde for our customers.
